## #4

$$
=
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 \\
2 & 0 \\
3 & -1 \\
0 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
a \\
b
\end{bmatrix}
$$
Those columns are linearly independent, so they form a 2 dimensional basis.
